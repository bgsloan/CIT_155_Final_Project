Check out our current events!

All guided hikes begin at the main entrance at 1945 Athens-Boonesboro Road.

Guided Hiking Schedule for Fall 2024
Fall Native Species Hike August 10th, 10 am – 12 pm Learn how to spot some of our most common native plant species in central Kentucky.
Birding in the Gorge August 17th, 8am – 12 pm Our knowledgeable volunteers will be leading this hike as we look for native birds along the lowest parts of the gorge along Lower Howard’s Creek
Salt Spring Trace Hike September 14th, 10 am – 12pm Join us for an interpretive hike about the history of the Salt Spring Trace. Wear your waterproof boots and be prepared to cross shallow water!
Haunted History Hike October 26th, 1pm – 3pm Learn all about the haunted history of Lower Howard’s Creek! We will be sharing folk tales and ghost stories from the area. Appropriate for children 8 years and older.
LHC Birthday Hike November 27th, 1pm – 3pm Join us as we celebrate LHC’s birthday! Refreshments will be served.

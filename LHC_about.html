About Us

Lower Howard’s Creek Nature and Heritage Preserve (LHC) is in Clark County in the heart of Kentucky’s Bluegrass region. Comprised of 441 acres along Lower Howard’s Creek, this preserve is home to federally endangered and state-designated rare plants.

Interested in Kentucky plants?

In addition to native natural resources, LHC is home to several historically significant man-made structures including the remnants of a mill, a homestead, stone fences, and roadways. Due to its location on the confluence of the Kentucky river, this area was vital to the growth of Clark County and Winchester as a city.
